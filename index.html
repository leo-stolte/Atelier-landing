    003 — How We Work

    A Process
    Built Around
    Objects

    We believe that great visualization begins with deep understanding — of the object, its context, and the story it needs to tell. Our process is collaborative, iterative, and always object-first.

    01

    Discovery & Brief

    We begin by understanding your objects, your brand language, and the experience you want to create.

    02

    Environment Design

    We build the spatial context — architecture, light, materials — that gives your objects their stage.

    03

    Render & Refine

    Iterative rendering cycles until every shadow, reflection, and material reads exactly right.
